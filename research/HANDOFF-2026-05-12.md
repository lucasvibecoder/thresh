## Four strategic observations (these reshape the playbook)

1. **AI-SDR pain has shifted (2024→2026):** "domain collapse" → "AI burns time without booking meetings." The 90-day kill curve is yesterday's pain. EDP for AI-SDR segment must change.
2. **Stack-fragmentation is a new segment** — buyers run 4–5 outbound tools; pain is orchestration, not vendor failure.
3. **Buyers misdiagnose their own root cause.** Jasper Vanu (LinkedIn): *"teams look at low reply rates and think 'we need better copy.' So they throw AI at it. Reply rates go from 0.5% to 0.7% and they call it progress."* → Thresh's cold email should *re-diagnose for them*, not start from their stated problem.
4. **Data quality is the unifying root cause** under all 6 themes. Quote Q-165 (Reddit): *"once you automate list building, you realize the bottleneck was never there anyway. it's still data quality."* This may be the single strongest meta-EDP across the harvest.
