## Messaging Notes by Sub-niche

**Recruiting (#1-3):** Lead with driver OOS rate and inspection count. Diagnostic question focuses on screening/qualification breakdown. Don't mention vehicle OOS unless it compounds the story.

**Dispatch / TMS (#4-7):** Lead with inspection volume and OOS frequency. Frame as "routes breaking" and "loads getting rescheduled." These founders think in fleet utilization and revenue-per-mile — connect driver OOS to lost revenue, not compliance risk.

**Trucksafe (#8):** Most natural fit — their entire business is fixing what the data exposes. Lead with Conditional safety rating + CSA score language. Mention specific violation categories (Vehicle Maintenance, Unsafe Driving, HOS) if available. Brandon Wiseman would understand this data instantly.

**OpenEyes (#9):** Lead with crash data and fatal incidents, not OOS rates. Insurance companies think in claims frequency and severity. "12 crashes including 1 fatal" hits harder than "52% driver OOS" for this audience.

**Koffie (#10):** Do NOT position as "data they don't have." They pull FMCSA. Position as "prospecting intelligence they haven't thought to use this way" — carriers in acute distress who will be shopping for new insurance when their current carrier non-renews.
