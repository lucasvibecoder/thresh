## Failure-mode scan

- **Target/register:** factoring credit/risk vocab throughout. ✓
- **AI-SDR pattern:** no LinkedIn quote, no fake observation. ✓
- **Forced analogies:** none. ✓
- **Telescoping:** system-level. ✓
- **Machinery before curiosity:** one line. ✓
- **Jargon:** all in recipient daily vocab. ✓
- **Dual CTA:** opening question is rhetorical (not a CTA — no "let me know how" prompt); closing CTA is single. ✓
- **Premature attachments:** none. ✓
- **Question-led risk:** could read as consultant-y / SDR-y. Mitigated by question being highly operational ("how do you flag X today") and immediately backed with the stat. Tone stays peer-to-peer, not survey-bot. ✓

## Decisions intentionally NOT taken

- **No "be honest" or "curious" preamble** before the question — those soften the opener into AI-SDR territory.
- **No follow-up qualifier** ("and what does that workflow cost you?") — that would tip into discovery-call register. The question is a setup for the stat, not a real ask.
- **Question phrased as workflow ("how does your team flag X")** rather than pain ("are stale MCS-150s causing issues?"). Workflow question respects that recipient may already have a workflow; pain question assumes problem.
